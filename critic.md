- **Preachy Vocabulary**: "rapidly evolving," "revolutionize industries," "immense benefits," "significant risks"

## Global Improvement Strategies

### 1. Kill These Patterns Across All Posts
- **Remove**: "In conclusion," "Ultimately," "In summary"
- **Replace**: "--" with em-dashes (—) or commas
- **Delete**: "It's important to note that," "We should consider"
- **Avoid**: "Let's examine," "Let's explore," "Think of it like"

### 2. Add Human Elements
- **Personal anecdotes**: "I ran into this last week when..."
- **Strong opinions**: "Honestly, I think..." instead of "It could be argued that..."
- **Contractions**: "it's" instead of "it is," "don't" instead of "do not"
- **Varying sentence lengths**: Mix short, punchy sentences with longer ones

### 3. Structural Changes
- **Break the sandwich**: Don't always end with summary
- **Vary bullet point lengths**: Some one-liners, some detailed
- **Remove perfect parallelism**: Make sections different lengths
- **Combine related sections**: Don't force artificial separation

### 4. Voice Injection
- **Use "I" statements**: "I've found that..." instead of "One might find..."
- **Add specific examples**: Real projects, real problems
- **Include contractions and natural language**
- **Remove hedge words**: "could be," "might," "potentially" (unless necessary)
